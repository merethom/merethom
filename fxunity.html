fxUnity Mobile Web Application

OANDA fxUnity was a retail forex trading platform targeted to beginner forex traders. The fxUnity platform lets users trade baskets of currencies, rather than currency pairs, to gain a feel for the forex market while incorporating a self-contained social network.

The Request

Work with a project manager and user experience designer to create a mobile-web version of OANDA’s fxUnity platform. Trading interactions must be easy to complete for new traders on mobile devices.

The Idea

Extend OANDA’s brand to create a friendly/gamified atmosphere for the application. Use clean and simple graphics to make forex trading seem more approachable to beginner traders.

The Solution

The mobile site draws on colour palettes and iconography from OANDA’s brand standards, and was designed to maintain similar interactions to the web version of the platform.
