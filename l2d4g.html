#L2D4G: A Personal Manifesto of Design Ethics

As an activity, I believe design is any and all things done to negotiate between reality and that-which-could-be [possibility]. Possibility refers to anything which is imagined and desired. What are the ethics of this negotiation?

The Request

I was disappointed at the lack of information available on design ethics. So, I did what any young, well-meaning creative does: I went back to school to get a masters degree to find out more.

The Idea

For my masters dissertation I developed an ethical guide I can aspire toward in my design practice. It is meant to provide broad and fluid—not fixed and rigid—guide for my work. It is meant to give me hope. Read the Full Text of L2D4G

The Solution

What I’ve created is undoubtedly deeply personal, but I have grounded it in enough theory hold up to academic rigour and hopefully even contain some takeaway or inspiration for others who read it.
