OANDA Website Redesign

OANDA Corporation is a financial services provider of currency conversion, online retail foreign exchange (forex) trading and forex information. They believe currency exchange should be transparent and fair.

The Request

Redesign the OANDA family of websites to improve brand cohesion. The redesign must make use of a tabbed navigation and be flexible enough to support A/B testing and product line changes.

The Idea

Use colours and iconography taken from the fxTrade platform as a reference point for the redesign. Rather than a radical change, opt to make the update feel like a logical evolution of the brand. View OANDA.COM

The Solution

A site with tabbed navigation and unified page header/footer (where the previous elements were different on each site) that allows customers to quickly find the content they’re looking for.
