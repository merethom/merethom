Sales Materials for OANDA fxTrade

Marketing a retail foreign exchange broker in the Asia Pacific market requires large amounts of face-to-face communication between customer and broker. Strong print materials are a great takeaway for customers after these interactions.

The Request

Design a set of modular components that can be used by the sales team in a variety of contexts—one-on-one client, trade shows, etc.—including A4 sales sheets that can be printed as-needed.

The Idea

Develop a cohesive modular visual language that can be applied to a variety of print deliverables. Reinforce the idea that OANDA’s brand is both innovative, technology-driven, and trustworthy.

The Solution

The visual direction conveys aspects of trustworthiness and technological excellence while working within the branding standards through the use of clean typography, imagery and data visualization.
