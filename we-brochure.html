We: Guide for Live-In Caregiver Program

For far too long caregivers have been victims. Our message to them is simple: they have rights. It does not matter who they are up against, the government will protect them.

The Request

Looking to the Centre for Urban Pedagogy for inspiration, create a brochure that uses information design to communicate complex information that is beneficial to society but is currently not displayed in an accessible manner. The deliverable should be A4 sized when folded and open to reveal and A1 poster on the verso.

The Idea

This pamphlet is a comprehensive guide that outlines worker rights and employer responsibility in Canada’s Live-In Caregiver program (LCP). This piece aggregates all of the government information about the program in one place, and is intended for distribution by Citizenship and Immigration Canada and worker support agencies.

The Solution

An easy-to-read typeface and approachable colour palette make the document more accessible to the reader. The information graphic on the verso of the document maps out the program’s entire process beginning with the decision to become a caregiver, and ending with application for permanent resident status.
